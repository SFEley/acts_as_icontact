ActsAsIcontact::ContactHistory

The read-only list of actions attached to every Contact. Because of this intrinsic association, the usual find methods don’t work; contact history must be obtained using the individual contact’s history method. Property updates and saving are also prohibited (returning a ReadOnlyError exception.)
