ActsAsIcontact::MessageClicks

The read-only list of clicks attached to every Message. Because of this intrinsic association, the usual find methods don’t work; this collection must be obtained using the individual message’s clicks method. Property updates and saving are also prohibited (returning a ReadOnlyError exception.)
