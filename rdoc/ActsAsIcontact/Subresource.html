ActsAsIcontact::Subresource

A read-only resource placed beneath the URL of another resource and containing secondary information. Because of this intrinsic association, the usual find methods don’t work; subresources must be obtained using an association method from the parent resource. Property updates and saving are also prohibited (returning a ReadOnlyError exception.)
